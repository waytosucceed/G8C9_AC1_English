Adverbs and their types.


Overview of the Grammar topics:

ADVERBS are those words in sentence that modify the following:

  • VERBS
  • ADJECTIVES
  • ANOTHER ADVERB

Adverbs have various types:

  • In a sentence, Adverbs of Place describe/define the place of action. They answer the question “Where is the action happening?”.
  • In a sentence, Adverbs of Time tell us the time of the action. They answer the question “when an action happened?”
  • In a sentence, Adverbs of Frequency indicate the rate at which something/some action happens, that is the number of times an action takes place.
  • In a sentence, Adverbs of Manner answer the question, how an action was conducted or it can also describe another adjective.
